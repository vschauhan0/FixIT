RATING SYSTEM (Real Calculation)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Formula: completedJobs × 0.2
Starting Rating: 0
Increasing Per Job: +0.2
Maximum Rating: 5.0

Examples:
  0 jobs completed → Rating 0.0
  1 job completed  → Rating 0.2
  5 jobs completed → Rating 1.0
  10 jobs completed → Rating 2.0
  25 jobs completed → Rating 5.0 (maximum)

Updates: On job completion
Persistence: Across sessions


REVENUE SYSTEM (Real Calculation)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Source: Actual booking prices
Calculation: Sum of all completed booking prices
Updates: On job completion
Persistence: Across sessions

Example:
  Job 1: ₹400 proposed → Mark completed → Revenue: ₹400
  Job 2: ₹500 proposed → Mark completed → Revenue: ₹900
  Job 3: ₹300 proposed → Still pending → Revenue: ₹900 (unchanged)

Display: Real value in worker dashboard (₹{actual})
